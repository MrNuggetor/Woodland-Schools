Discipline Policy

Clear standards for behavior and accountability.

We expect our pupils and students to respect themselves and other people at all times. They must remember they are ambassadors of Woodland Hills and so are obliged to be of good, sensible behaviour both in and outside the school premises. Students are expected to greet staff with their titles and names while all visitors must be addressed as "Sir" or "Ma". No student should be involved in any matter involving money.

Bullying in any form will not be tolerated.

The use of mobile phones, iPods, laptops and other non-academic electronic devices is not allowed. Cheating during tests, assignments, homework, project or examination is prohibited. Offenders shall be punished.

Disciplinary measures which are mainly detentions and in more serious cases suspension will be a way of reinforcing the discipline expected.

School Bell

Daily schedules for Secondary and Primary sections.

High School

Students are expected to report to school not later than 7.45am. There shall be morning assembly on the assembly ground at 7.45am prompt. The school gate is finally shut at 8.00am. Latecomers' names will be recorded. Afternoon assembly will be held daily at 4.30pm to mark the close of day for all students.

On Mondays to Thursdays, classes begin at 8.00am and end at 4.30pm. On Fridays, classes begin at 8.00am and end at 2.50pm.

Primary School

Pupils are expected to report to school no later than 7.45am. There shall be morning assembly on the assembly ground at 8.00am prompt. The school gate is finally shut at 8.00am. Latecomers' names will be recorded.

On Mondays to Thursdays, classes begin at 8.15am and end at 3.00pm. On Fridays, classes begin at 8.00am and end at 1.00pm.
