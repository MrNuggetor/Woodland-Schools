About Woodland Hills High School

Balanced education for students ages 10 to 16.

Woodland Hills High School is a co-educational private school which endeavours to provide a balanced education for students between the age of ten (10) and sixteen (16). We strive to accomplish this through the provision of a qualitative Nigeria/British education.

The Federal curricular, Lagos State Scheme of work and relevant British syllabi is taught so as to empower the students with the knowledge required to ensure they succeed and achieve the highest standard of academic success according to each student's ability.

Our curriculum is subject to constant review and will definitely take care of each individual's interest, talents and skills.

We aim at making the children aware of their own potential and to instill in the children the importance of self-pride and confidence, so that they can stand as equals with their counterparts from any part of the world.

For us at Woodland, learning is not about what you get, it is about what you become.

Our Mission

The school embarks on a mission to:

  1. Encourage pupils and students to work hard in all they do.
  2. Provide excellent education whereby students can succeed and achieve the highest standard of academic success.
  3. Develop each student's character and personality to become God fearing, responsible, sensible, self disciplined members of the society.

Our Vision

To prepare pupils and students for a life of leadership, service and personal fulfilment.

In a bid to achieve our mission, we ensure that our staff are qualified individuals who not only love and understand children but are also firm believers in our school's motto, "Hard work is all".

We also know that children are the future and the promise of the future is an awesome promise, so we try to bring out the best in each child both morally and academically by giving them encouragement and teaching them well so that they can lead the way in our ever changing world.

This website provides information to questions you may have concerning our school policies and procedures. If you still have questions about anything, please do not hesitate to contact the school office.

We look forward to partnering with you as we endeavour to bring out the best in each child.
