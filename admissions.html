Admission Steps

Simple, supportive process for families.

1. Inquiry

Visit or call our admissions office to speak with a counselor.

2. Assessment

Placement test and an interview for learners and parents.

3. Enrollment

Complete documentation and receive your welcome pack.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for parents and prospective families.

What curriculum is taught?

We deliver the Nigerian Federal curriculum alongside Lagos State scheme of work and relevant British syllabi.

How do I apply?

Visit the admissions office or contact us to begin the assessment and enrollment process.

What support is available?

Pastoral care, guidance counseling, and remedial support are available for all learners.

Community & Values

We partner with families to build character, discipline, and leadership.

Character Formation

Respect, responsibility, and integrity are emphasized through daily routines and mentorship.

Students are encouraged to lead with humility and serve their communities.

Parent Partnership

We maintain open communication with parents through meetings, reports, and engagement sessions.

Our goal is to help every child thrive academically, morally, and socially.
